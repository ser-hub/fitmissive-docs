<h1>Welcome to Fitmissive</h1>
                    <p>
                        Fitmissive is a web application that allows you to track your workouts and share your results with your friends.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                        You can create an account and start tracking your workouts.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                        You can also search for other users and see their workouts.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                        You can also see your own workouts and share them with your friends.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                        You can also see your own profile and edit it.
                    </p>
                    <p>
                        You can also log out.
                    </p>
